The main objective is to train future professionals who are studying Professional Training Degrees in the area of sustainability, taking as a point of reference the calculation and reduction of the carbon footprint.

There are 7 main goals: • Train students and teachers in calculating the carbon footprint. • Reduce CO2 emissions from academic centers by 30%. • Inculcate 'green skills' and make the academic environment, both teachers and students, aware of the concept of climate change (its causes, consequences and actions we can take to help stop it). • Exchange of good practices between educational centers (especially between students) at an international level. • Social and gender inclusion involving 100% of the students, with and without special needs. • Involve all partners and related companies in the most important activities on calculation and reduction of carbon footprint. • Design new environmental policies and sustainability plans in academic centers.

The project has an initial duration of 2 years and 3 months: September 2020- December 2022. Due to the pandemic an extension has been requested and the project will end in April 2023.

The project is directed by the Cuatrovientos Integrated Center and is also made up of the following educational centres: CENIFER (Higher Integrated Center for Renewable Energies), Technologische Fachoberschule Bozen, HTBLA Wolfsberg, Göranssonska Skolan and the consultancy STENUM Unternehmensberatung und Forschungsgesellschaft fur Umweltfragen MBH.

2 Spanish partners: CI CUATROVIENTOS AND CENIFER, based in Pamplona (Navarra). 1 Italian partner: Technologische Fachoberschule Bozen, based in Bozen (Alto Adige – South Tyrol) Translated from Spanish to English - www.onlinedoctranslator.com 2 Austrian partners: HTBLA Wolfsberg, based in Wolfsberg (Kärnten) and STENUM, based in Graz (Steiermark) 1 Swedish partner: Göranssonska Skolan, based in Sandviken (Gävleborgs län)
